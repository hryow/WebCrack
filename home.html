What it is

This extension is a tool to be used for help in detecting bias within articles. It determines how biased an article may be, suggests articles with alternative perspectives, and approximates the length of the article in minutes.

Why we built it

Our bias detector is tailored for ESL (English as a second language) individuals, since the language barrier makes it difficult for them to be able to completely and accurately identify bias and objective reporting. By providing the means to recognize bias, our platform does more than just improve language proficiency; it provides them with the critical thinking skills needed to traverse the complex digital information landscape. This is not just a tool; it is an incentive for empowerment designed to enhance linguistic abilities and intellectual perception in the face of bias.

Features

Bias Detection

The extension checks the background of both the source and author, as well as the language use within the article.

Suggested Reading

The extension will estimate the amount of time that it will take for the reader to completely read through.

Length Approximation

The extension will suggest additional reading that may present an alternate perspective.
