Features

Bias Detection

The extension checks the background of both the source and the author. It also analyzes the language use within the article to also determine if any bias is present.

Suggested Reading

The extension will suggest additional reading that may present an alternate perspective. For example, if an article is heavily biased towards one view, the extension will suggest an article that is strongly biased towards another view.

Length Approximation

The extension will estimate the amount of time that it will take for the reader to completely read through the article.
